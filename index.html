Galactic Archaeology

I am an observational astronomer studying the oldest stars and stellar streams in the Milky Way. Our Galaxy is my laboratory and metal-poor stars are my time machines.

My work spans catalogue creation, distance derivation, isochrone fitting, stellar streams, spectral data reduction and analysis, photometry and spectroscopy of metal-poor stars, and astrostatistics. I combine Gaia, the Pristine survey, spectroscopic surveys, and follow-up observations from telescopes in La Palma.

I was born and raised in Chennai, India. I earned my BE from Anna University, my MSc in Astrophysics from the University of Glasgow, and my PhD in Astronomy from the University of Groningen with Else Starkenburg and Amina Helmi.
